Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty, oven-baked Italian dish layered with flat pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. The pasta sheets are typically layered with a robust Bolognese sauce made from ground beef, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and Italian herbs, simmered until thick and flavorful. Each layer is generously topped with a creamy béchamel sauce and sprinkled with a blend of cheeses, often including mozzarella and Parmesan, which me lt to form a golden, bubbling crust. The dish is baked until the flavors meld together, resulting in a satisfying, comforting meal with a perfect balance of textures—tender pasta, rich sauce, and gooey cheese.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Tomatoes
  • Anything else you would like!
  1. Make sauce
  2. Cut tomatoes
  3. Grate cheese
  4. Mix ingredients together
  5. Bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees
